How to play:

  • The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible by shooting zombies that are coming towards you.
  • Use the arrow keys to move and rotate your player on the screen. You can move forward, backward, left or right.
  • Press the spacebar to shoot bullets at the zombies. You can only shoot one bullet at a time.
  • Each zombie has a health of 3 and can be killed by three bullets. You get points for killing zombies, depending on their size. Bigger zombies give more points than smaller ones.
  • The zombies will spawn randomly from the edges of the screen and will chase you. They will also get faster and more frequent as you score more points.
  • You have 3 lives to start with. You lose a life if a zombie touches you. You also become invulnerable for a short time after losing a life.
  • The game is over when you lose all your lives.
  • You can pause and resume the game by pressing the escape key.
